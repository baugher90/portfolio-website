About

Professional Background

I'm focused on creating products that users will love. My background in sales and marketing taught me that LESS is more. I bring that focus to my development projects. I strive to write the simplest coed that will solve the problem. in order to do that I make sure that I completely understand a problem before I try to solve it. This leads to better longer lasting code that is easier to maintain.
